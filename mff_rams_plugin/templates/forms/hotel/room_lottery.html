{% if show_dealer_info %}

Dealer Room Block Information

A block of rooms is set aside exclusively for Dealers at the Crowne Plaza, just across from the convention center. We strongly recommend anyone dealing at MFF include the Crowne Plaza in their hotel choices. Doing so is not required, and we will still award a room in a higher-ranked choice if one is available just as we do for any other attendee, but this will dramatically increase your chances of obtaining a room.

If your dealer application is pending at the time you enter, that is okay. We will check all dealer applications before announcing room results.

Room groups must have a dealer or dealer assistant as the group leader to be eligible for the dealer block. You may have one room in the dealer block for every three assigned and completed dealer or dealer assistant badges on your application.

We encourage people from the same dealer group to room together to help accommodate as many people as possible.

{% endif %} {% if show_staff_rates %}

Staff Room Block Information

Our hotel contracts set aside a certain number of rooms for staff, sometimes at a reduced rate. Your room lottery entry will first go into the staff lottery, where we only fill blocks at staff rates (when applicable). If all rooms are exhausted in a hotel's staff block, no more rooms will be booked in that hotel for the staff lottery.

After the staff lottery closes and rooms are awarded, you will be given the option to enter the attendee lottery. Staff in the attendee lottery will be placed in a staff block if rooms are still available after the staff lottery. Otherwise, rooms are awarded out of the attendee block with the attendee rate.

Should your entry be selected during the attendee lottery, it is both possible and probable the room will be awarded out of the attendee block at the attendee rate.

We have a wide variety of ADA-compliant rooms which can accommodate those with mobility, hearing, or visual impairments. Note that {{ c.EVENT_NAME }} will likely receive more ADA requests than available rooms; once ADA rooms are exhausted, standard rooms are awarded.

At the conclusion of the lottery, MFF Accessibility Services reaches out to all room awards with an ADA request, regardless of the awarded room type. If an ADA request is made but the available rooms cannot accommodate your needs, your reservation may be canceled without penalty.

If you do not require an ADA-compliant room, but would like to request an accomodation for medical need(s) (e.g., quiet floor placement, low floor placement, close proximity to elevators/stairs), please leave the checkbox below unchecked. Instead, if you are awarded a room after the lottery's conclusion, please reach out to MFF Accessibility Services to discuss available options. If the available rooms cannot accommodate your needs, the reservation may be canceled without penalty.

Please note: At this time, no intra-hotel shuttles are equipped with wheelchair lifts.
