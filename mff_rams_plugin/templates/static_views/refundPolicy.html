Midwest FurFest Badge Policies

When Can I Pick Up My Badge?

Badges may be picked up at Registration in Hall A of the Donald E. Stephens Convention Center during the following times:

Thursday, November 30, 2023:

3PM - 5PM - Sponsor and Shiny Sponsor Preregistration Only
5PM - 7PM - Preregistration Attendees Only
7PM - 10PM - All Preregistration and At The Door Attendees

Friday, December 1, 2023:

10AM - 8PM - All Preregistration and At The Door Attendees

Saturday, December 2, 2023:

10AM - 6PM - All Preregistration and At The Door Attendees

Sunday, December 3, 2023:

10AM - 3PM - All Preregistration and At The Door Attendees

All attendees will be required to show ID to a Registration staffer in order to receive a badge. In addition, some minors may be required to have additional paperwork. Minors and Parents should read our Parent's and Minor's Guide for a complete set of questions, and forms which may be required.

What is the Midwest FurFest Refund Policy?

Refunds for your Midwest FurFest registration can be requested by sending an email to {{ "registration@furfest.org"|email_to_link }}. Refund requests must be sent from the email address associated with the registration account requesting the refund. All refunds will be sent to the original payment method.

Attendees can request a full refund through 11:59PM Central Standard Time on Saturday, December 9, 2023.

Sponsors and Shiny Sponsors can request a full refund through 11:59PM Central Standard Time on Wednesday, November 29, 2023. A partial refund equal to the amount of the pre-registration Attendee registration can be requested until 11:59PM Central Standard Time on Saturday, December 9, 2023.

Any badge that is marked as Checked In or Picked Up on Thursday, November 30, 2023 or later is not eligible for a refund.

What Do I Do If I Have Other Questions?

Please, first check out our Frequently Asked Questions page. Many common questions have been answered there.

If you have any other questions not covered by the FAQ, please reach out to {{ "registration@furfest.org"|email_to_link }} and we will be happy to assist you.
