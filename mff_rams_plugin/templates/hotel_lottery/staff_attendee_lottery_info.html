Please read the following before entering the attendee lottery:
  • If you received a room in the Hyatt Regency O'Hare in the staff lottery, you may ONLY enter for a suite in the attendee room lottery, not a standard room. {% if application.entry_type == c.ROOM_ENTRY %}
    • If this applies to you, please make sure to switch your entry type to a suite entry by pressing the "Change to Suite Entry" button above your application information after entering the attendee lottery.
    {% endif %}
  • If you are awarded a room in the attendee lottery, the room you were awarded in the staff lottery will be automatically cancelled. {% if application.room_group_name and application.group_members %} This will also cancel the room for all your room group members! {% endif %}
    • To be very clear, entering the attendee lottery will not cancel your room from the staff lottery, but you will NOT be able to choose between an attendee lottery room and your staff lottery room. Please make sure to only select preferences that will be better than your staff room award!
